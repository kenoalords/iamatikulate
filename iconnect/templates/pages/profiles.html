Atiku Abubakar (GCON)

Former Vice President of Nigeria (1999-2007)

Alhaji Atiku Abubakar GCON (born 25 November 1946) is a Nigerian politician and business tycoon. He served as Vice President of Nigeria from 1999 to 2007 alongside President Olusegun Obasanjo

Alhaji Atiku Abubakar attended Jada Primary School, then moved on to Adamawa Provincial Secondary School in Yola where he completed his WASSCE/GCE Certificate in 1965. He attained a Diploma in Law from Ahmadu Bello University Institute of Administration.

Peter Obi

Former Governor of Anambra State, Nigeria (2006-2014)

Peter Obi was born on 19 July 1961 at Onitsha. He served as the Governor of Anambra State from 2007 to 2014.

Peter Obi attended Christ the King College, Onitsha where he completed his secondary school education. He was admitted to the University of Nigeria, Nsukka in 1980, graduating with a B.A (Hons) in philosophy in 1984.
