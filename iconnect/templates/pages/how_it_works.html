How it works

Introduction

iamatikulate.com is an online community of dedicated supporters of Atiku Abubakar presidential ambition in the upcoming 2019 general elections in Nigeria.

This platform was created as a collaborative social tool to shed more light on the challenges of the average Nigeria and proffer solutions to problems currently plaguing our dear nation.

How to use iamatikulate.com

Sign up for an account if you haven't already done so and start identifing and sharing problems in your local communities to help HE. Atiku Abubakar better understand our problems and how best to tackle them.

Once you've verified your E-mail address, you can start sharing your expectations or challenges with HE. Atiku Abubakar.

Why do you require my location?

We only require an approximate location information to help us understand your post better in regards to the region you're currently posting from.

We only collect information such as City, State and Country.
